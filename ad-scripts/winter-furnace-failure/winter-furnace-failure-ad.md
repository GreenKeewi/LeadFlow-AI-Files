# LeadFlow — Winter Furnace Failure
## 15-Second Meta Feed Video Ad

**Platform:** Meta Feed (Facebook / Instagram)
**Length:** 15 seconds
**Goal:** Drive homeowners to find their nearest HVAC tech before winter
**Tone:** Urgent and trustworthy — not salesy, not gimmicky
**Production Tool:** Google Flow (Imagen + Veo)

---

## THE SCRIPT

---

### SECONDS 0–3 — THE HOOK

*Tight close-up on a dark bedroom. Condensation on a window. A digital thermostat reads 58°F. The frame is cold — desaturated blues and grays.*

**VO:** "Your furnace will fail on the coldest night of the year."

**Text on screen:** YOUR FURNACE WILL FAIL.

---

### SECONDS 4–9 — THE PROBLEM

*Wide shot of a living room. A mother pulls a blanket tighter around her child on the couch. The overhead light flickers. A space heater glows orange in the corner. Outside the window — winter.*

**VO:** "Half of all HVAC breakdowns happen in January and February — when every tech in your area is already booked solid."

**Text on screen:** Half of all HVAC failures happen in peak winter. Most techs are already booked.

---

### SECONDS 10–13 — THE SOLUTION

*POV shot of a phone screen. A clean interface shows a map with a pulsing pin — a certified HVAC tech 4 miles away. A match confirmation slides up. Warm green tones cut through the cold color grade.*

**VO:** "LeadFlow connects you to the nearest certified HVAC tech — in seconds, not days."

**Text on screen:** Nearest certified tech. Connected in seconds.

---

### SECONDS 14–15 — THE CTA

*The app match screen holds. A bold CTA button pulses on screen.*

**VO:** "Find yours before winter hits."

**Text on screen:** Find Your HVAC Tech Now →

---

**Total VO word count: 47 words**

## OUTPUT 5 — TARGETING & TIMING NOTE

Run this ad from **October 15 through February 28** — the window when furnace anxiety is highest and HVAC demand spikes. The sweet spot is early November (just before the first hard freeze in most markets) and again in late January when the post-holiday freeze hits and homeowners start noticing heating inconsistencies they ignored in fall.

On Meta, layer:
- **Homeownership signals** — Facebook's "Likely to move" and "Homeowners" categories
- **Age:** 28–55
- **Household income:** $60K+
- **Interests:** home improvement, home maintenance, Angi / HomeAdvisor engagement
- **Behavioral:** people who have recently searched or engaged with heating/cooling content
- **Geo:** ZIP-code-level targeting in regions with average January lows below 30°F — Upper Midwest, Northeast, Mid-Atlantic, Mountain states

Use a **$15–25 CPM bid** with a **3-frequency cap per week** — after 3 views the fear-based hook loses its punch and risks ad fatigue. Retarget anyone who watched **50%+ of the video** with a testimonial or urgency variant reminding them that booking slots fill in winter. They've already felt the fear — now close them.
