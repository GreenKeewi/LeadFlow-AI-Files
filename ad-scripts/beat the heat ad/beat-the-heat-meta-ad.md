# Ad Script — "Beat the Heat"

**Platform:** Meta Feed (Facebook / Instagram)
**Length:** 22 seconds
**Target:** Homeowners, ages 25–55, warm/hot climate regions
**Goal:** Drive clicks to LeadFlow landing page → lead captured → routed to nearest HVAC SP
**Aspect Ratio:** 4:5 or 9:16 (feed + Reels)

---

## Script

| Time | VO / Text on Screen | Visual |
|---|---|---|
| **0–3s** | *(no VO)* | Scene 1 |
| **3–8s** | *"If your AC breaks this summer..."* | Scene 2 |
| **8–14s** | *"...most homeowners wait 2–3 days for a callback."* | Scene 3 |
| **14–18s** | *"We connect you to a verified local HVAC tech — fast."* | Scene 4 |
| **18–21s** | *"Same-day service. No runaround."* | Scene 5 |
| **21–22s** | **"Get matched now →"** *(CTA button)* | Scene 6 |

---

## Scene Prompts
> **Workflow:** Each scene uses a two-step Flow by Google pipeline — generate a static image first with the Image Prompt, then animate it into video with the Flow Prompt.

---

### Scene 1 — Hook (0–3s)

**Image Prompt (Imagen)**
Cinematic close-up of a digital thermostat mounted on an interior wall displaying 84°F. A person's bare forearm is visible in the lower frame with a single bead of sweat forming on the skin. Harsh afternoon sunlight streams through a nearby window, casting a hot, bleached haze across the room. Photorealistic, shallow depth of field, warm overexposed tone. No text, no logos.

**Flow Prompt (animate the image)**
Slow, creeping push-in toward the thermostat display. The sweat bead on the arm slowly travels downward. The overhead light flickers once and dims slightly, suggesting the AC unit straining. Subtle heat shimmer in the sunlit area. Duration: 3 seconds.

---

### Scene 2 — Pain Point (3–8s)

**Image Prompt (Imagen)**
A frustrated homeowner in their early 30s to mid 40s, wearing a casual t-shirt and shorts, sits slouched on a beige couch in a modern suburban living room. They are holding a smartphone and fanning themselves slowly with a folded piece of paper, a tired and resigned expression on their face. Bright sunlight blazes through slatted blinds in the background, casting harsh lines of light. Photorealistic, warm amber color grade.

**Flow Prompt (animate the image)**
The paper fan moves back and forth in slow, exhausted arcs. The homeowner's shoulders drop slightly as they glance down at their phone. Background light through the blinds shifts subtly as if a cloud briefly passes, then returns bright. Duration: 5 seconds.

---

### Scene 3 — Agitate (8–14s)

**Image Prompt (Imagen)**
A clean split-screen composition on a warm amber/orange background. Left side: a smartphone screen showing a missed call notification and a voicemail alert from "HVAC Company." Right side: a monthly calendar with three consecutive days circled or crossed out and the label "Next available" written in red beside the circled date. Flat graphic style with photorealistic phone. Warm, frustrated color palette.

**Flow Prompt (animate the image)**
The voicemail notification on the phone screen pulses and blinks as if a new message just arrived. On the calendar side, each of the three waiting days highlights and dims in sequence — one, two, three — driving home the delay. Subtle vignette darkens the edges. Duration: 6 seconds.

---

### Scene 4 — Solution (14–18s)

**Image Prompt (Imagen)**
A close-up of a smartphone being held in one hand, screen facing forward. On the screen: a clean, minimal mobile form UI. The question reads "What service do you need?" with "AC Repair" selected and highlighted in emerald green (#059669). Below it, a zip code field is filled in. The progress indicator reads "Matching you to the nearest available tech..." Crisp, bright, high-contrast visuals. Emerald green accents on white background.

**Flow Prompt (animate the image)**
The progress bar fills from left to right over 2 seconds. The status text transitions from "Matching..." to a bold green checkmark and the words "Tech found nearby." The emerald highlight on the selected option glows gently. Subtle bounce on the checkmark. Duration: 4 seconds.

---

### Scene 5 — Proof/Benefit (18–21s)

**Image Prompt (Imagen)**
A uniformed HVAC technician in a clean branded shirt and work pants stands at the front door of a suburban home, tool bag in hand, smiling. The homeowner — casual clothes, visibly relieved expression — stands in the doorway greeting them. Warm golden afternoon light, manicured front yard visible behind the technician. Photorealistic. Color grade transitioning from warm amber to cooler, neutral tones to signal relief.

**Flow Prompt (animate the image)**
The technician takes a step forward and the homeowner opens the door wider, both exchanging a brief nod. The color temperature of the scene subtly shifts from warm amber to cooler, refreshing neutrals — visually representing the heat breaking. Duration: 3 seconds.

---

### Scene 6 — CTA (21–22s)

> **Note:** Scene 6 is a static card — no Flow animation needed.

**Image Prompt (Imagen) / Static Card**
A clean, full-bleed card on emerald green (#059669) background. Centered: the LeadFlow wordmark in clean white sans-serif. Below it, bold white text reads "Get matched now." A simple white right-pointing arrow or pill button sits beneath the text. Minimal, high-contrast, no gradients. Designed for maximum legibility at small sizes on a mobile feed.

---

## Production Notes

- **Captions on:** ~85% of Meta video is watched without sound — burn in all VO as on-screen text
- **Hook rule:** Scene 1 must stop the scroll in under 1 second — no logos, no intros
- **AI tools:** Use **Flow by Google** for all video generation. Pipeline: Image Prompt → Imagen → static image → Flow Prompt → animated clip. Scene 6 is a static card only.
- **Color palette:** Emerald `#059669` for brand moments; warm amber for pain scenes; cool/neutral for resolution
