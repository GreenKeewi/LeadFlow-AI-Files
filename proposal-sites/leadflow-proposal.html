Intelligent Routing

The Right SP Gets Every Lead. Automatically.

When a customer submits a request, LeadFlow finds the best match in your network in seconds — no manual dispatching, no leads sitting unassigned.

1

Customer selects service type

AC Repair, Furnace, Installation, or Maintenance — determines which SPs in your network are eligible.

2

Customer enters postal code or city

Location is geocoded instantly — LeadFlow begins calculating distance to all service providers in real time.

3

Nearest available SP is selected

If the closest SP is unavailable, the system automatically cascades to the next best match.

4

Lead fires into the shared CRM instantly

Full record — name, contact, service, location, channel source — delivered to the SP with zero delay.

No manual dispatching. No leads sitting unassigned.

Revive Campaigns

Most Systems Stop at Delivery. Ours Doesn't.

LeadFlow's re-engagement layer turns cold leads and past customers into repeat revenue — automatically, without your SPs lifting a finger.

Cold Lead Re-engagement

Leads that didn't convert are automatically flagged and re-entered into a follow-up sequence via email or SMS — until they book or opt out.

Past Customer Follow-Up

Customers who previously booked are automatically contacted for repeat business — annual reminders, seasonal checkups — on autopilot.

Seasonal Promotions

Your SPs' customer bases receive automated seasonal outreach — "Get your AC checked before summer," "Furnace tune-up before winter" — timed perfectly.
